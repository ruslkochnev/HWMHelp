Quests

Unfortunately quests are available only in RU-version of game.


Quests - one of the elements of the game, which is a variety of plot tasks. During their passage you will encounter different tasks, riddles and puzzles, in the course of solving which they can get acquainted more closely with the world of the game, its features, characters and history, and also save the Empire from another threat. Depending on the nature of the gameplay quests are text and combat.

Text quests are a description of events with the subsequent choice of actions. During the passage, you can find some intralobject items that you can use or connect with each other. During the quest you will be awarded points (the maximum possible number of points for the quest is 100). If you do not do something, you will get fewer points, and therefore the reward will be less. The first pass of the task is awarded with gold depending on the points scored. The number of replay is unlimited, but you will not get a reward for re-passing. Successful completion of the quest opens new tasks. To quit the quest and return to normal gaming sessions, you can at any time by using the "Quit Quest" button in the form of a white flag, in which case the quest will have to pass from the beginning.

Combat quests unlike text can be taken from any corner of the Empire, entering the building on the game map. Another distinguishing feature of these quests is that initially the character is credited with 100 points, and the main task in passing is not to lose them. In combat quests, points are deducted for frequent defeats in the same battle (over 2 defeats). Quest battles in no way depend on the development of the character and the artifacts on it, and for their conduct are not scored points of skill or experience. In addition, such battles will not affect the character's statistics. Artifacts in quest battles do not break down, and attempts to win are not limited. Any fight can be completed in advance by clicking the "Surrender" button. Also at any time you can use the "Exit" button, and then continue the quest from the same place, the points you collect will not be written off.
