Insignia

Tiger Lake medals
Medals of three grades for exemplary military service, personal courage and valor shown during the Empire's campaign at Tiger Lake.

Defender medals
Tokens of distinction of five grades for bravery and urge for victory in the war with the Undead at Wolf dale.

Medals of courage
Medals of three grades for courage and valor shown in quenching the riot in Empire lands.

Orders of courage
Tokens of distinction of six grades awarded to soldiers of the Empire for courage and valor shown in:
-fulfilling military duties in battles with combined armies of elves and dark elves in Green wood and Lizard lowland;
- carrying out special missions when fighting the "lizard epidemic".


Orders of Freedom
Orders of six grades for courage and valor shown in liberating the Empire from Demon invaders.


Orders of Peace
Orders of five grades for courage and valor shown in battles against hostile Wizards.

Medals of Confrontation
Medals of five grades for courage and valor shown in confronting the thieves.

Imperial insignia
Imperial awards of seven grades for courage and valor demonstrated in struggle against Dwarves.


Medals of seven grades for courage and valor demonstrated in strife with the Kingdom.


Orders of seven grades for courage and valor shown in combats against the Tribals and enemies from the Past.


Imperial decorations
Three types of decorations awarded for certain feats in the war with the Dwarves.

Three types of decorations awarded for certain feats in the war with the Kingdom.

Three types of decorations awarded for certain feats in the war with the Tribals and enemies from the Past.
