Roulette

Game purpose
The croupier launches a ball on a revolving wheel that is divided into 38 sections, each marked by a number: 00, 0, 1 through 36.
The purpose is to guess, which section the ball stops at. The winning depends on how the bets were made.

Stakes
Roulette stakes are divided into nine types. Each type covers a certain set of numbers and has its own winning scale. The roulette spins every 10 minutes.
The players get their winnings proportionally to their victorious bets.

Players can view the history of last 3 hours' spins to check the results.

Minimal stake is 25*[Combat level] gold (at least 100 gold), maximal one equals 1000*[Combat level] gold.
Bet type Description Winning rate
Straight Bet on a certain number of 1 through 36 and both Zero (0, 00) directly. The chip is placed on the number tile directly. 35 to 1
Split Bet on two adjacent number tiles on the board. The chip is placed on the line separating those tiles. Note that you can also bet on the line separating the Zeros (between 0 and 00). 17 to 1
Street Bet on three numbers. Place your chip on the outer board part just across the street of three numbers you want to bet on. 11 to 1
Corner This stake covers four adjacent number tiles. Place the chip on the crossing where all 4 tiles are joint. 8 to 1
Fiveline This stake covers both Zeros (0, 00) and numbers 1, 2, 3. The chip should be placed on the top crossing of tiles 00 and 3. 6 to 1
Sixline Betting on sixline covers two adjacent streets (which is, six different tiles in two columns. E.g.: 4,5,6 + 7,8,9). To bet this way, place your chip on the crossing of outer border line and the line that separates the two streets. 5 to 1
Column There are three tiles on the left side of the board, marked as "2to1". By placing a chip onto one of them, you bet on all 12 numbers that are situated to its right (excluding Zeros). 2 to 1
Dozen There is another way to bet on sets of 12 numbers. To do so, place a chip onto one of the tiles marked as "1st 12", "2nd 12", and "3rd 12", which correspond to the first, second and third dozens respectively. 2 to 1

Fair game control system.
A long number sequence has been created using a random number generator. That sequence predetermines the roll results in roulette games.

Every month the players will be granted access to a password-protected archive with the sequence for the current month. In the beginning of the next month, the password will be proclaimed.

This system guarantees the randomness and invariability of game results.

Roulette results are 100% random and fair, any player can certify that after the lapse of each month.
